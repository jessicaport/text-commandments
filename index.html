The Text Commandments

  1. Thou shalt not allow autocorrect blunders to embarrass thyself.
  2. Recognizing that others have a life, thou shalt not expect immediate responses to text messages. When an immediate response is not forthcoming, thou shalt refrain from sending additional texts.
  3. Thou shalt clearly label sarcastic texts with "jk", "haha", or with an appropriate emoticon.
  4. Thou shalt understand the incredible nausea inducing potential of emoticons, and refrain from their overuse.
  5. Thou shalt neither instigate nor promote text arguments ("textguments").
  6. Thou shalt label as cowards those who dump a significant other through texting.
  7. Thou shalt not text thy parents nor grandparents unless they initiate texting with you, instead preferring to call them regularly.
  8. Thou shalt not drunk text, unless texting another drunk.
  9. Texting shall not be relied upon solely to arrange first dates.
  10. Thou shalt honor the company of thy mother and father, family and friends by not texting while in their presence, that your days may be long.
